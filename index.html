What is the purpose of such organizations?

The United Nations and other NGOs are some of the biggest advocates for social justice. Read to see how they take action to help combat these issues, as well as see how they can use your help.

The Rohingya Refugees

400,000. It is believed that at least 400,000 Rohingyan refugees are crossing the border to Bangladesh, fleeing from Myanmar to escape murder, persecution, discrimination, and destruction. These people are desperate, doing whatever they can to escape from the Burmese military and government. It has been noted that the core of the violence has been in the Rakhine state, where groups march into villages, shouting as one refugee reported, “leave, or we will kill you all,” In this state, the government in Myanmar makes deliberate, accurate attacks against the villages in this state, many homes are burned down, and families are torn apart. The people of Rakhine state do not have access to education, healthcare, the basic necessities and liberties everyone should have. This is a humanitarian emergency. As many of these refugees are fleeing to camps in Bangladesh, it is crucial to start an integration process so that refugees have an opportunity to restore their ways of life.

Cameroon, Africa

As many people do not know, since 2016 Cameroon has been through some violent changes.Cameroon has two different official languages english-speaking people were now as Anglophone and French Speaking people were known as Francophone. United Nations and NGOs

How can these organizations help?

The United Nations + the Rohingya Refugees

The United Nations Refugee Agency, also known as the UNHCR works hard to improve the conditions for refugees all over the world. Seeing that the Rohingya Refugee Crisis is the fastest growing refugee emergency in the world, the UNHCR has been providing emergency aid and supplies to help save lives. Supplies includes sleeping mats, plastic sheets, and kitchen sets. The UNHCR helps more than 250,000 refugees and pours about 7.8 million dollars into aid. The UNHCR has also partnered with companies working toward helping the water situaton within the region. With this help, pipe lines were able to be built to get the refugees fresh water. Also, latrines were constructed to create more humane conditions for these people.

NGOS and their role in the refugee crisis

Humanitarian Practice Network

The Humanitarian Practice Network, also known as the HPN works hard to put a stop to the refugee crisis in Bangladesh. NGOs like HPN serve as mediums between the common man, the UN, and the people in need. For example, HPN fundraises supplies as well as spreads awareness about hte problem at hand. Like most NGOs, HPN has the common passion for solving such a crisis, but their main issue is funding. They don't have the resources to give endless amounts of supplies and invest money into building these people new lives. That being said, it is important that the common man gets involved in helping either the NGO with their work, as well as through donations.
